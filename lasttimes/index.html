Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Last Times ("the App") handles your data. Last Times was designed to respect your privacy — all personal information stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

1. Information We Collect

Last Times does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information you enter into the app — such as your age, relationship details, or time preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults system.

If you enable iCloud or backup your device, your data may be securely stored and encrypted by Apple as part of your iCloud backup.

2. No Account or Login Required

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any identifiable information. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

3. Data You Choose to Share

If you decide to share screenshots, insights, or other app-generated content, that information is only shared through the channels you select (for example, Messages or social media). The app itself does not upload or transmit your data automatically.

4. Third-Party Services

Last Times currently uses no third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking frameworks. If this changes in a future version, the Privacy Policy will be updated and you will be notified before any new data is collected.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will remove all app data permanently. You can also reset all app data at any time from the settings menu inside the app (if provided).

6. Children's Privacy

Last Times is not directed toward children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect improvements or changes in app functionality. The updated policy will always be available at this URL with an updated effective date.
